Instructions

Welcome to the 2048 Game! Here's how to play:

  1. Use the arrow keys or swipe gestures to move the tiles.
  2. When you move a tile, a new tile with a value of either 2 will appear in a random empty spot on the board.
  3. If two tiles with the same value collide when moved, they will merge into a single tile with the sum of their values.
  4. The goal is to create a tile with the value of 2048 by merging tiles.
  5. The game ends when you can no longer make any moves.

Remember, strategic placement of tiles is key to achieving a high score! Pay attention to the entire board and plan your moves accordingly.

Good luck and have fun!
